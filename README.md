Project Title:

6-Month Sales Performance Comparison Dashboard – Rishabh vs Jahnvi
 

Objective:

To create an interactive Power BI dashboard that compares the monthly sales performance of two sales representatives Rishabh and Jahnvi across six months.
The dashboard helps analyze who performed better, identify trends, and understand sales consistency.
 

Time Period:

January 2025 – June 2025

•	## Overview
•	This Power BI project compares the sales performance of two sales representatives, Rishabh and Jahnvi, over a six-month period. The dashboard visualizes key metrics such as total sales, profit, target achievement, and month-wise growth.
•	## KPIs
•	- Total Sales (6 months)
•	- Average Monthly Sales
•	- Total Profit
•	- Target Achievement (%)
•	- Month-wise Sales Growth
•	
•	## Visualizations
•	- KPI Cards
•	- Line Chart: Monthly Sales Trend
•	- Column Chart: Profit Comparison
•	- Bar Chart: Target vs Actual
•	- Pie Chart: Sales Contribution
•	
•	## Tools Used
•	- Power BI
•	- DAX (for KPIs)
•	- CSV Dataset
•	
•	## Insights
•	- Both salespersons show consistent growth over 6 months.
•	- Rishabh slightly outperformed Jahnvi in total sales.
•	- Jahnvi had higher profit consistency.
•	- Target achievement above 95% for both.
